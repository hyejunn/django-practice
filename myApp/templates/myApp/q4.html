4. Find the model and price of the printer of the highest price of each maker

query statement : "SELECT printers.model, price
 FROM products, printers
 WHERE products.model = printers.model AND price IN (
     SELECT MAX(price)
     FROM products, printers
     WHERE products.model = printers.model
     GROUP BY maker)"
